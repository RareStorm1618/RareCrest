Non IP Program Costs
Staff time at RareStorm (family coordinators, community managers).
Outreach, training, research, evaluation.
Compliance audits, legal review, insurance.
All three are standard nonprofit expenditures and do not require RareStorm to own the underlying platform IP.

Your Program Director salary at RareStorm.
Any service / subscription fees RareStorm pays to Roderick E Bentley holding company.

6. What Not to Do
Given your risk tolerance and the stakes, avoid:
Retroactive “sales” of IP from RareStorm to you.
That invites questions you do not need to answer.
Having RareStorm directly fund core platform patents or generic infrastructure going forward.
That muddies the background IP story and creates valuation headaches at spin out.
Letting grant language say or imply that “RareStorm is building and owning a new platform” when in fact it is funding access/implementation on a founder owned platform.
Grant narratives should be crystal clear that RareStorm is:
Deploying an existing, founder created platform, and
Funding seats, implementations, and program operations.
