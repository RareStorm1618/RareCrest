===== PAGE 2/9 =====
The Rare Storm 501(c)(3) nonprofit was founded to:   
 
1. Promote research into rare diseases.   
 
2. Foster collaboration in rare disease research efforts.   
 
3. Provide support to families grappling with the challenges of rare 
diseases. 
 
It serves as a vital link between compassionate aid and scientific progress, 
acting as a crucial bridge to seamlessly connect charitable support and 
scientific innovation. 
 
The name Rare Storm comes from an inspirational untitled poem that 
describes battling evil as a storm. Our daughter’s name, Abri, means 
shelter. "Shelter from the Storm" carries dual meanings: It signifies both 
guarding against the storm and providing protection from it.
