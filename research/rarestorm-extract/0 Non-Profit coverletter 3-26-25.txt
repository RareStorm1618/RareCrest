The Rare Storm 501(c)(3) nonprofit was founded to:  

Promote research into rare diseases.  

Foster collaboration in rare disease research efforts.  

Provide support to families grappling with the challenges of rare diseases.

It serves as a vital link between compassionate aid and scientific progress, acting as a crucial bridge to seamlessly connect charitable support and scientific innovation.

The name Rare Storm comes from an inspirational untitled poem that describes battling evil as a storm. Our daughter’s name, Abri, means shelter. "Shelter from the Storm" carries dual meanings: It signifies both guarding against the storm and providing protection from it.
