## WHY THIS DECISION MATTERS

**The Challenge:** Rare disease families are suffering from isolation, fragmented healthcare, and lack of research access. RareAngels is a comprehensive digital platform (CircleOfSupport, RareConnect, RareRate, RareMarketplace, clinical trial matching, blockchain transparency) that directly solves these problems.

**The Opportunity:** By sponsoring RareAngels as a nonprofit pilot, RareStorm can:

1. **Accelerate development** (no startup delay; immediate funding access)
2. **Access grants** (nonprofits access $50K–$2M rare disease grants; for-profits cannot)
3. **Protect families** (nonprofit governance, HIPAA/FDA compliance oversight, board accountability)
4. **Benefit from success** (if pilot proves viable at Month 12, RareStorm retains a perpetual charitable license and ongoing financial benefit)

**This is urgent.** Families are waiting.

## QUESTIONS?

**Likely Q&A:**

**Q: "Is this legal?"**  
A: Yes. This is an IRS‑accepted fiscal sponsorship and related‑party IP license structure, used nationwide. RareStorm retains a perpetual charitable license and oversight. Roderick receives only reasonable compensation, and any commercial IP transaction must be at fair market value. Legal counsel can review before you vote.

**Q: "What if Roderick steals the code?"**  
A: He cannot remove or revoke RareStorm’s perpetual charitable license. The IP License & Ownership Agreement and Fiscal Sponsorship Agreement explicitly protect RareStorm’s rights. Any commercial transaction must be documented, at fair market value, and cannot extinguish RareStorm’s charitable use rights.

**Q: "What if the pilot fails?"**  
A: Foundation loses the $500K investment and time. But families benefit from the learning; RareStorm retains its perpetual charitable license to the platform and can continue any mission‑critical use or wind down responsibly. Board controls the decision at Month 6 and Month 12.

**Q: "Won't this distract from other Foundation work?"**  
A: RareAngels is a separate project with its own budget and team. It doesn't touch other Foundation programs. This is additive, not distracting.

**Q: "What if families' data is breached?"**  
A: We have cyber insurance, annual security audits, HIPAA compliance, and encryption. If a breach occurs: immediate notification, forensics investigation, family notification. Board is notified immediately.
